What is hack{cyprus}*12?

In short, the first ever weekend hackathon in Cyprus, in September 2012.

We're excited about it and you should be too. Right now, we are trying to prove that people want this to happen as much as we do. You can help by clicking the button above — think of it as adding your "signature" to this event. The more we have, the easier it will be to approach potential sponsors, mentors and media partners.

Help us bring an exciting weekend of hacking to Cyprus — we'll make this happen together.

What can you expect at the hackathon?

Code

Build, break-it-better, build

Hack on your ideas using any technology you like. Meet other people and form teams. We'll provide drinks, food and internet so you can focus on just building cool stuff. We'll also try to organise 15-minute talks on various technologies throughout the weekend, so we can all learn while hacking as well.

Sun

Tech + good weather = l33t

If you live abroad, forget rain and clouds. We have 326 days of sunshine every year. When you're done with coding, you can hit the beach in no time. During the event, we'll make sure that it's always cool and nice, and we'll provide plenty of refreshments, always, free of charge.

Prizes

Rewards for your hard work

Sponsor-permitting, we also want to give out prizes to the best ideas, prototypes and implementations. We haven't worked out the details yet, but presentations will probably take place during the last hour of the event on Sunday. A team of judges and your own input will determine the winners. All these over beers!
